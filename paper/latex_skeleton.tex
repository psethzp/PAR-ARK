\begin{abstract}
Adaptive retrieval over text-rich knowledge graphs requires both broad entity discovery and multi-hop relational exploration. We introduce PAR-ARK, a GPU-local extension of ARK with profile-conditioned action priors and trace-state repair. Experiments on STaRK show improvements over same-backbone open-weight ARK under matched compute, while reducing wasted graph actions.
\end{abstract}
